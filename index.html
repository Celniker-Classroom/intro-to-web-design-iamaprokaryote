STEVEN CHEN

Studio/Gig Musician Based in San Diego, California

About Me

Steven is a studio and gig musician from San Diego. He plays saxophone and piano, and he has experience with all kinds of genres of music. He started as a classical pianist, and by fourth grade, he picked up the saxophone. Since then, he has embodied both instruments and performs regularly on each. He also leads the JazzUp SD Club, which sponsors student musicians to showcase their talents through concerts for the community.

About the Combo

The JazzUp SD Combo is a group of talented student jazz musicians who came together to keep jazz thriving. The combo started as a trio, with saxophonist Steven Chen, pianist Miles Krawitz, and drummer Casey Phan. Since then, it has grown to include pianist Sameer Prakash, Bassist Liam Putnam, and drummer Atticus Previte. The combo can be seen performing at various concerts and galas across San Diego. You can book the combo at your next private event by contacting JazzUp SD through their website or by email and requesting a quote.

Performance Experience

Steven has performed in various venues worldwide. He made his Carnegie Hall debut in 2023 on piano and has since returned to perform on saxophone. He was also the lead alto in both the California All-State and All-Southern honor bands. Steven has won awards at various local, regional, and international competitions, including first place in the Charleston, London, and American Protege competitions. He was recently awarded by the prestigious Coastal Communities Concert Band Don Caneva Memorial scholarship competition.
